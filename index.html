Zifeng Wang

I am a Research Scientist at Google Cloud AI Research, focusing on large language models (LLMs) and their applications. My research interests span model adaptation, multi-LLM collaboration, and multi-agent systems. I received my PhD in Machine Learning from Northeastern University's SPIRAL Group, advised by Prof. Jennifer G. Dy, with close collaboration with Prof. Stratis Ioannidis and Prof. Yanzhi Wang. Prior to my PhD, I earned my BS in Electronic Engineering from Tsinghua University, where I worked on computer vision with Prof. Jiwen Lu (Tsinghua) and Prof. Jia Deng (Princeton), and big data with Prof. Yong Li (Tsinghua).

I am looking for self-motivated student researchers / research interns with interests and expertise in LLMs! Feel free to contact me if you would like to work on cutting-edge challenges in machine learning and LLMs.
